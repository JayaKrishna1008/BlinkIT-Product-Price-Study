# BlinkIT-Product-Price-Study

## Project Objective
The objective of this project is to perform a detailed analysis of BlinkITâ€™s grocery dataset to understand how online grocery items are categorized, priced, and distributed across various brands. Through data cleaning, exploration, and visualization, the goal is to uncover key insights and trends that reflect consumer choices, product availability, and pricing strategies in the online grocery market. ## Questions (KPIs)
-Which outlet has the highest total sales?
-What percentage of items have 'Low Fat' as their fat content?
-Which outlet has the highest total sales across all items?
-How does outlet size impact overall sales performance?
-Which item type has the highest average rating?
-Do higher-rated products generate more revenue than lower-rated ones?
